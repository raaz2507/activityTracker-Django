Activity Tracker App

This app is a personal productivity / learning logger, where users can track their daily activities and gain insights about their time usage.

  1. Models Structure

    • Users can track study time, sports, movie watching, gaming, book reading, and many other activities.
    • Users get to know when and which activity they did, and for how long.
    • Activity records will store: activity source, reason/trigger, start time, end time, and date.

  2. Main Features

    • Add Activity – Log new activities easily.
    • View Activities – Check activities in calendar or list view.
    • Graphs & Charts – Visualize progress with pie charts and bar charts.
    • Analytics – Analyze how much time was spent on each activity and which source had the highest engagement.
    • Custom Choice Fields – Predefined activity sources and triggers using TextChoices.

  3. Future Enhancements

    • Export activity data as CSV/Excel.
